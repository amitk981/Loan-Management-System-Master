# Risk Assessment

Risk level: High

- Changes portal object scope, legal evidence provenance, schema, upload bytes, and document access.
- Scope derives exclusively from active PortalAccount membership; cross-member/internal access is
  nondisclosing and creates no success evidence.
- Upload history is append-only/immutable, centrally stored, checksum-bound, and cannot mutate
  checklist, signature, security, custody, bank, readiness, loan-account, or disbursement truth.
- Current downloads are limited to current-renderer Term Sheet/Loan Agreement and require a second
  authenticated portal request; bytes are checksum verified and separately audited.
- Residual risk: local browser screenshots were sandbox-blocked. Independent browser acceptance is
  required; no owner veto or revoked approval applies.
